Karachi Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 pound chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Coriander leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the basmati rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes.
  2. In a pot, combine the chicken, onion, garlic, ginger, tomatoes, yogurt, biryani masala, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and let it marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Drain the soaked rice and add it to the marinated chicken mixture. Add enough water to cover the ingredients.
  4. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook over medium heat until the rice and chicken are fully cooked, about 25-30 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and let it rest for 10 minutes.
  6. Fluff the biryani with a fork and garnish with coriander leaves before serving.
