Pakistani Goat Liver

Ingredients

  • 1 pound goat liver, cleaned and sliced
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a pan, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add the garlic and ginger, and sauté for another minute.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes, red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add the sliced goat liver and mix gently to coat it with the spices.
  5. Cover the pan and cook over low heat for about 10-12 minutes, or until the liver is cooked through.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
